Business advisory

Numbers your board and bank can rely on — not just a spreadsheet export

We help owners and finance leads build a clear view of cash, working capital and profitability before big decisions: hiring, capex, debt, or a change in structure. Deliverables are written so you can circulate them internally without re-explaining the model.

Where we add leverage

Advisory is most valuable when it connects management reports, tax outcomes and risk — not when it stops at a slide deck.

◇

Cash & working capital

13-week cash views, covenant-style metrics and “what breaks first” stress tests before you commit to spend.

▣

Management reporting

Consistent month-end packs: revenue bridges, margin drivers and KPIs your team can own.

◎

Structure & growth

High-level options for expansion, divestment or refinancing — with tax and compliance flags called out early.

Typical deliverables

  • Board-ready memo with assumptions and limitations
  • Excel or cloud model you can refresh each quarter
  • One-page decision summary for owners
  • Handover notes for your bookkeeper or CFO

What to prepare

  • Last 3–6 months of management accounts and bank statements
  • Budget or forecast, even if rough
  • List of upcoming decisions (e.g. hire, asset purchase, loan)
  • Any lender or investor templates you need to satisfy
