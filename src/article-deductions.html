Individuals

Deductions many individuals overlook

22 September 2025 · 3 min read

The ATO allows deductions only where expenses are directly related to earning income, you were not reimbursed, and you can substantiate the claim. “I always claim this” is not a defence — but many taxpayers still miss legitimate items because they did not keep evidence.

Work-related expenses

Uniforms with logos, protective equipment, union fees and professional subscriptions may qualify when tied to your employment. Home office costs have specific methods and eligibility rules; copying last year’s claim without checking current guidance is risky.

Investment and property

Interest, management fees, repairs (not improvements) and borrowing costs can be relevant for investments — each with timing and apportionment rules between private and income-producing use.

Records beat estimates

Bank statements alone are often insufficient. Invoices, receipts, diaries for motor vehicle and home office usage, and rental statements should be filed as you go. Digital photos of receipts fade — use a proper app or folder structure.
