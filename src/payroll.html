Payroll

STP, super and awards — configured once, reconciled every pay cycle

We work with Australian employers on pay codes, leave accruals, STP Phase 2 reporting and superannuation guarantee reconciliations. The goal is clean data flowing to the ATO and your BAS preparer — not emergency fixes at year-end.

What we run end-to-end

From first hire through to complex pay structures — we document decisions so your team can repeat the process confidently.

◇

STP & pay events

Pay code mapping, termination payments, allowances and corrections with YTD checks before you submit.

▣

Onboarding & awards

New starter checklists, modern award classifications and rate tables maintained with change logs.

◎

Monthly payroll run

Reconciliation of net pay, PAYG withholding, super liabilities and clearing accounts before you approve payment.

Typical deliverables

  • Payroll procedure note for your internal HR or finance team
  • Month-end reconciliation pack (PDF + working file)
  • Super clearing house or fund choice documentation
  • Year-end payment summary / STP finalisation checklist

What to prepare

  • Employee master file: TFN declarations, contracts, base rates
  • Current award or enterprise agreement references
  • Last three pay runs exported from your payroll system
  • List of allowances, reimbursements and salary sacrifice arrangements
