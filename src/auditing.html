Audit & assurance

Independent assurance that stands up to lenders, members and regulators

We perform financial report audits and agreed-upon procedures for Australian entities that need a clear opinion or findings report. Engagements are partner-led with documented testing, sampling and communication to those charged with governance.

How we structure assurance work

Whether you need a full audit or a narrower procedure, we scope materiality, risks and timelines up front — then keep you updated as fieldwork progresses.

◇

Financial report audits

Testing over revenue, payroll, journals and estimates with tailored workprograms for your industry.

▣

Agreed-upon procedures

Targeted procedures for lenders, grants or joint-venture partners where a full audit is not required.

◎

Reporting & closing

Clear communication of misstatements, control observations and deadlines for management responses.

Typical deliverables

  • Audit planning memo and timeline
  • Independent auditor’s report (where applicable)
  • Management letter with control and process notes
  • Closing meeting notes and signed representation letter pack

What to prepare

  • Trial balance, general ledger and supporting schedules
  • Bank confirmations and related-party agreements
  • Fixed asset register and depreciation policy
  • Prior-year audit file and management responses (if any)
