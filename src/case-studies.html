Case studies

How we structure work when timelines, compliance or growth are on the line.

Tax & BAS · Multi-site retail

Reconciling BAS after a messy handover

Situation

A growing retailer had inconsistent GST coding, unreconciled clearing accounts and looming BAS deadlines after changing bookkeepers mid-year.

What we did

We mapped transactions by site, aligned chart-of-accounts rules with POS exports, and worked through prior quarters with a clear evidence trail for the ATO.

Outcome

Lodgements back on schedule, fewer surprises at EOFY, and a short monthly checklist the internal team could own.

Payroll · Professional services

STP Phase 2 without disrupting pay runs

Situation

A 40-person firm needed pay codes and leave types updated for STP Phase 2 while maintaining fortnightly payroll.

What we did

We staged changes between pay cycles, validated YTD figures against prior reporting, and documented award interpretation notes for HR.

Outcome

Clean STP reporting, streamlined onboarding for new hires, and payroll confidence ahead of audit season.

Advisory · Family business

Cash-flow visibility before a refinance

Situation

Directors wanted a simple forward view of liquidity before speaking with their bank about facility terms.

What we did

We linked management reports to actuals, stress-tested assumptions, and aligned dividend policy with working-capital needs.

Outcome

A board-ready pack, clearer decision rules for capex, and fewer ad hoc “what’s left in the account?” conversations.
