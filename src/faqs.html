Frequently asked questions

Straight answers on tax, GST, payroll and how we work with clients across Melbourne — no jargon for the sake of it.

Every situation is different. If you don’t see your question here, contact us — we’re happy to point you in the right direction.

Tax & individuals

When do I need to lodge my personal tax return?

Most individuals must lodge by 31 October after the end of the financial year unless you’re using a registered tax agent — in that case, eligible clients often have a later due date. We’ll confirm your timeline once we’re engaged.

What records should I keep if I work from home?

Keep evidence of hours worked, expenses paid and how you calculated the claim. The ATO publishes specific methods for home office deductions; we help you choose a method that fits your records and stays compliant.

Do I need to report crypto or other investments?

Often yes — capital gains, income and certain transactions may need to be declared. We can map your activity to the right labels and avoid surprises at lodgement.

Business, GST & BAS

When do I need to register for GST?

Generally when your GST turnover exceeds the annual threshold (or you elect to register voluntarily). If you’re close to the threshold or sell certain types of goods or services, we’ll review your numbers and timing.

How often is BAS lodged?

Most businesses lodge monthly or quarterly depending on turnover and registration. We align preparation with your bookkeeping so you’re not rushing before each due date.

What’s the difference between a company and a sole trader for tax?

Structure affects tax rates, liability, reporting and compliance. We explain trade-offs in plain language and work with your lawyer or refer you to one when legal or structural advice is needed.

Payroll, STP & super

What is Single Touch Payroll (STP)?

STP sends payroll and super information to the ATO each pay run. Employers must report through compliant payroll software. We help set up your payroll stack and keep reporting consistent.

When are super guarantee contributions due?

Super must be paid at least quarterly by set deadlines. Late payments can trigger penalties and the super guarantee charge. We build calendar reminders into your payroll rhythm.

Working with Constant & Co

How do I get started?

Most clients begin with a short call or contact form so we understand your needs. We’ll outline scope, fees and timelines before any work begins.

How do you share documents securely?

We use encrypted channels and our client portal where appropriate so you can upload tax documents and records safely. Ask us if you’re unsure what to send.

Do you only work with businesses in Melbourne?

We’re based in Melbourne and work with many local clients. Depending on your needs, we may also support remote or interstate clients — contact us to discuss.
